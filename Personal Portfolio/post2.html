Pune Property Price Prediction System

This was a project my two friends and I are working in our 3rd year of college.

The system stores different aspects of property such as total sq. feet, no. of bathrooms, and what locality its located in.

Then by using our trained machine learning model, we are able to accurately predict the price of buying a flat in that location in accordance to the deatils given.
